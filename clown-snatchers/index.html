Clown Snatchers

Clown Snatchers

The circus just got into town, but all the clowns got lost!

Clown spawning

In this Gamejam project I was in charge of the clown AI. First part of this was spawning. I made a tool to make clowns spawn anywhere in the level where a navigation mesh was present, in a customizable radius. The level designers could use this to change how many clowns, how fast, and where they spawned as was needed.

Walking and animation

Because of the limited timeframe of the project, we wouldn’t have time for actual animations. To give the clowns a bit of charm, I proposed the idea to have the clowns rotate akin to children bean toys. This idea went trough and was seen as a great addition to the vibe of the game.

Collisions

The main mechanic of the game was to capture the clowns with your van. If you didn’t hit at the right angle, the clowns were supposed to react. I added a spherical collision to the clowns, and when they get hit they use this to fly and roll away, and get back up when they’ve come to a stop afterwards.
