Cry of the Fox is an action adventure game set in a post apocalyptic Paris. You play as a fox, who has to find and sneak their way through wolf infested streets. My work was on the AI of the wolfs that search for you and chase you throughout the game.

Ornament Express is a VR puzzle game on Steam. You are a hired detective, and play through an escape room like experience. The player has to puzzles in the environment using pieces found around a train carriage. My work was on player interactions and puzzle experience, validated through playtesting.
